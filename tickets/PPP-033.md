# Outcome

The final submission film preserves visual continuity from each real request
through its real provider wait and into the resulting product. Standalone
`Generation time compressed` cards are removed; only the waiting portion of
the original real-Codex footage is accelerated with a small speed disclosure.

# Scope

- Replace every standalone compression card with the remaining tail of its
  corresponding real prompt/wait capture.
- Play those real wait tails at 4x speed while keeping the initial prompt and
  resulting product footage at normal speed.
- Keep a small `4x` disclosure over accelerated footage without obscuring the
  product or conversation.
- Remove the obsolete compression slide from the deck and build pipeline.
- Rebuild and revalidate the final MP4, subtitles, representative frames, and
  documentation.

# Acceptance Criteria

- [x] No `Generation time compressed` card appears in the final film.
- [x] Every shortened wait is real PPP-025 footage played at 4x speed.
- [x] Request, progress, activation, and outcome remain visually continuous.
- [x] The acceleration disclosure is legible and does not hide product state.
- [x] The final film remains below 180 seconds with the required media streams.
- [x] Narration and subtitle timelines remain synchronized and monotonic.
- [x] Generated-product footage remains real-provider-only and secret-safe.

# Domain/PBT Tests

- Assert the build source has no compression-slide function or scene 09.
- Probe and fully decode the rebuilt MP4.
- Validate subtitle monotonicity and final endpoint against media duration.
- Extract representative accelerated-wait frames and the full contact sheet for

- Run the repository lint, formatting, secret scan, and complete release gate.
